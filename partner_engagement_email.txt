**Concept**
The rapid expansion of the Internet of Medical Things (IoMT) has created a pressing need for robust data privacy and security. Current cloud-centric models often expose sensitive patient data to risks such as data poisoning and unauthorized access. Our solution is a **Next-Generation IoT (NG-IoT) framework** that integrates **Edge Intelligence** with **Blockchain technology**. By processing data locally at the edge (on-device or near-device) and using blockchain for decentralized, tamper-proof verification, we can ensure real-time, privacy-preserving analytics. This approach not only secures patient data but also reduces latency and bandwidth usage, making it ideal for critical remote patient monitoring applications.
